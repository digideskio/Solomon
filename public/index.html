        Creating a Dispute

        To create a new dispute, type in a unique name for your dispute and hit Get Separated. Try to make it short, pithy, and memorable. It has to be unique, so if we already have a dispute of that name stored, you will be mocked for your lack of creativity and prompted to create a new name.

        TIP: Remember the name of your dispute! You'll need to send it to the other party.

        Finding an Existing Dispute

        To find an existing dispute, enter the name of the dispute exactly, and click Find. If we can’t find a dispute with that name, you will be cordially invited to correct your error or create a new dispute.

        Creating a List

        This is the list of items that are being disputed. Before creating a list, make sure that both parties are in mutual agreement on the items being disputed. This list can only be made once, and can't be edited once you click Finalize.

        Reordering Items

        Once the list of items is created, both parties need to rank the items. First, enter your name in the name field, so we know whose ranking this is.

        Then, click on the items and drag them around. Stuff you want the most goes on top; stuff you don’t care about so much goes on the bottom. Once you’ve ranked everything, click Separate us. You’ll be shown a sad animation of a breaking heart.
